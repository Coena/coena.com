Small businesses can usually be more flexible than their larger businesses, but they usually lack mature information systems. Coena Node provides a complete application for small businesses to communicate effectively using common information structures with bigger businesses.

 

 

Small, tough, and powerful

The Coena Node application will run on a normal PC within a small business. It will work with slow or fast internet connections. And it is powerful enough to run all the supply chain operations for the small business.

 

Connect to POS and Accounting

Coena Node can easily connect to other systems within the small business, such as a Point-of-Sale (POS) or Accounting system. Coena Node can selectively obtain or extract information from these systems and provide it to the network.
