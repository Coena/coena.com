In a business network, people work together to sell and deliver products to customers and to procure and obtain products from suppliers. They may share common knowledge about products or stock levels and have a view of who is participating in the business network in what roles. Business network software must support operations such as orders, inventory management and shipments. Business network participants must be able to share all or some of this information with partners like logistics providers, distributors, suppliers, sales agents and retail outlets.

 

What is Coena?

Coena is business network software. A business network unites several independent business partners together by providing social network and supply chain functionalities. You could also think of it as social ERP. We like to think of Coena as the swiss-knife for business networks. It can manage almost any product, fit into any process, and work with almost anyone with a computer. It is simple and easy to understand, and most users can use the tool with no training at all. Making the business network accessible to any business, big or small.

 

Who can use Coena?

Anyone with a modern computer and some sort of internet connection can use Coena. It will work with good or bad internet connections, and it will work on almost all operating systems. Coena's aggressive pricing strategy means that even the smallest businesses can afford to use Coena's state-of-the-art business network system.

 

How do I start using Coena?

Some of our solutions have you working in a few days. If you have existing systems, and you would like to integrate Coena, we typically can have everything working within a few weeks. Contact us today and we will work out the best solution with you.

 

What can you do using Coena?

  • Keep a comprehensive knowledge-base of all of your products
  • Share your product information with any of your business partners
  • Easily advertise your products worldwide
  • Communicate with your partners with integrated business messaging and online chat
  • Buy and sell products together with your business partners
  • Manage your inventory
  • Share your inventory in real-time with branches offices and business partners

 

Coena's key benefits?

If your business has people in different locations and uses different processes, and you have to work together, then Coena is for you. With Coena, you can:

  • Reduce mistakes by providing consistent information about your products and synching it with anyone in your network
  • Increase revenue by connecting with new business partners anywhere in the world
  • Reduce operating costs by managing your ordering and procurement processes, quotations and invoices
  • Improve decision making by accessing global marketplace information
  • Increase flexibility by connecting to any business process without changing your own
  • Reduce inventory by sharing stock levels in real time across locations
  • Improve your sales forecasts by providing real time distributed market information
  • Reduce IT development costs up to 40% by using our model driven architecture based on semantic web standards
  • Improve your data quality with our knowledge based storage system
