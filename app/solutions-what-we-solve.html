Work the way you need to

Different people have different responsibilities, tasks, and approaches to do their work effectively in a supply chain. Coena's flexibility gives each person in the supply chain control over what information they need to see, and how they use the information to do their work.

Business messages need to use common EDI standards.

When several businesses decide to collaborate, they must use common Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) standards for Business Messages. Thankfully, these standards are openly (ebXML, GS1) available to everyone. These standards must be implemented by all participants in the supply chain network. Coena provides a complete platform for EDI message integration for small businesses, and gives large businesses the adapters to talk in a common language in a network.

Some businesses don't have systems. What about them?

Some businesses, especially small businesses, don't have complete systems to process EDI messages for Orders and Purchases. They use spreadsheets, simple systems based on MS-Access®, or even pen and paper. Coena provides a complete system for small businesses to manage their product portfolio, inventory, and orders.

 

Businesses incapable of supply chain collaboration.

Larger businesses with old systems that aren't capable of automatically updating the network about product information, sending and receiving business messages, and managing real-time inventory use Coena's Enterprise Adapters to extract their legacy data, and make them supply-chain collaboration capable. This means they don't have to change their existing applications, and yet they can create a single-source of product information, send and receive EDI messages, publish their inventory information to their webshop and selected partners, and manage their orders online.

Some businesses have very mature systems.

Some businesses have very mature systems, which makes it easier for Coena to work with them and make them capable of interfacing with any business, big or small.

How are systems with different data structures handled?

Coena's semantic mapping technology makes it possible for several businesses using different data structures to align easily on a common language. For those that are used to speaking in an existing data structure, Coena adapts it to a common data structure that others can understand. For those that are new to the game, we provide a complete system so that you don't even have to deal with the language. You run your business the way you want to, and Coena does the rest.
