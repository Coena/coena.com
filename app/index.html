Making the network
your business
Coena makes business network softwareCoena makes
business network software

In business networks, independent businesses work together to sell and buy collectively. Or to share knowlegde, such as the realtime stock levels of products. A business network can be moderated using Coena Hub, which supports common business network processes. While Coena Node lets individual businesses - big or small - connect to the network.

For more information, see our solutions for large enterprises, small businesses, and network managers.

"Vers van de kweker" and Coena"Vers van de kweker"
and Coena

On the 24th of June 2012, 50 growers from the Netherlands officially joined their forces to sell products directly to consumers using the Coena Busines Network Platform.

Versvandekweker.nl (translated in English as "Fresh from the Grower") is a unique business-driven initiative of Dutch growers, farmers and local producers with the aim of delivering high quality, fresh, honest, healthy, tasty and lovingly grown products. Initially starting with 10 partners, the initiative grew to a total of 50 farmers, all connected by using Coena's state-of-the-art solutions for Agricultural Cooperatives.
